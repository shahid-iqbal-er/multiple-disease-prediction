Machine Learning-based Multiple Disease Prediction System

This web application provides risk prediction for select diseases using trained machine learning models. It is intended for educational and screening support only and should not replace consultation with healthcare professionals.

Model Accuracy

  • Heart (Logistic Regression): ~88.52% (test accuracy)
  • Liver (Logistic Regression): ~73.50% (test accuracy)
  • Kidney (Decision Tree): ~100% (test accuracy on this dataset)
  • Breast Cancer (Random Forest): ~97.37% (test accuracy)

Accuracies are based on held-out test sets and may vary with different data splits.

Conditions Supported by This Web App

Heart Disease

Heart disease describes a range of conditions affecting the heart and blood vessels, such as coronary artery disease, heart rhythm disorders, and structural abnormalities.

Key Symptoms
  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in neck, jaw, throat, or back
  • Weakness or coldness in arms or legs
Liver Disease

Liver disease affects the function of the liver, leading to metabolic and detoxification impairments. Conditions include hepatitis, cirrhosis, and fatty liver disease.

Key Symptoms
  • Yellowing of skin or eyes (jaundice)
  • Swelling in ankles, legs, or abdomen
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Persistent fatigue
Chronic Kidney Disease

Chronic kidney disease (CKD) involves gradual loss of kidney function, causing waste accumulation in the body. Severe CKD may require dialysis or transplantation.

Key Symptoms
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Loss of appetite
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • High blood pressure (hypertension)
Breast Cancer

Breast cancer originates from abnormal growth of breast tissue cells. Early detection significantly improves treatment success and long-term outcomes.

Key Symptoms
  • A new lump or mass in the breast
  • Changes in breast shape or size
  • Skin dimpling or puckering
  • Nipple changes or unusual discharge
